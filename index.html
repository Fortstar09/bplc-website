How to prepare for BPLC 1.0

To make the most of BPLC 1.0, take time to review the event schedule and set clear goals for what you hope to learn or who you'd like to meet. Bring a notebook or device for notes, dress smart-casual, and come ready to connect. A quick look at the speakers and topics beforehand can deepen your experience. Most importantly, arrive with an open mind, ready to learn, reflect, and grow as a leader.
