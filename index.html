About Beyond Pointers

Beyond Pointers leadership Conference [1.0] is a purpose-driven event created to redefine how young people like you understand and embrace leadership. At Beyond Pointers, we believe that leadership isn't just about the titles. It is responsibility, courage, and everyday influence. Through real conversations and shared experiences, you will discover that leadership begins with intention and lives through impact. BPLC 1.0 is a movement, and it starts with you.

Our Vision

To raise bold and intentional leaders who lead with clarity and make lasting impact in all spheres of influence, large or small. To redefine leadership beyond the noise and raise a generation of intentional changemakers.

Our Mission

To inspire, guide, and support you with the mindset, skills and meaningful connections you need to lead with purpose and make a difference.

How to prepare for BPLC 1.0

  • Get a standard jotter and pen or a working device to take notes.
  • Write out your expectations for the conference-no limits.
  • Come ready to connect, be open to meeting changemakers like yourself.
  • Dress smart-casual - be comfortable, and very presentable.
  • Do a brief Google search about the speakers before the D-day.
  • Come with a ready heart to learn, and learn you will!
